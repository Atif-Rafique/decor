Decorish interior design surpasses market expectations, creating desirable products which stand out from the market.

Interiors are the spaces most seen by users. While the design of the architecture may be elegant, the interior Design is what completes the feeling of quality. Our approach to interior design is trend-free. Trends are short-lived. Decorish is a firm believer in minimal and clean design with well resolved functionality will never go out of style. Elegance in proportions, high quality, right materials and composition are the directions Decorish follow. Minimalism is far more difficult to pull off, compared to the old fashioned approach of hiding everything with decoration. What was once considered an opulent style, is now greatly reduced in value because of the proliferation of crystal chandelier and Louis XIV furniture. Simplicity is the new luxury.

Clear, uncluttered spaces are the way forward. Standing out is impossible when following trends. Due to Decorish strong belief that timeless, minimal design is the priority, we create memorable, distinguished spaces which often are the exact opposite of trends. The end result is clear, subtle and beautiful. As a result the projects exude a feeling of luxury, regardless if the construction budget is high or low. Decorish interior design surpasses market expectations, creating desirable products which stand out from the market.
