Master Planning

Decorish designs master plans with the public realm at the forefront, resulting in well-connected road and pedestrian networks. This is accomplished in an aesthetically pleasing geometric framework, which is influenced by the necessities of creating a proper public realm with all the connectivity that this entails. The result is a functional, aesthetically pleasing, interconnected master plan, with maximised income potential.

One of the biggest concerns in GCC Masterplans is that many projects don’t create interconnected, flexible, and pedestrian-friendly roads. This kind of setup may exhibit some level of functionality, but definitely reduces the amount of income potential for the project. Consider a typical situation: In many places, crossing the street requires the one who wants to cross to drive and make U-turns. What could be a 2-minute walk, turns into a 15-minute drive, adding traffic to the roads.

Conversely, all the best cities on Earth have deeply interconnected road networks, and even in harsher climates such as winter in New York, their pedestrian networks see a steady flow of foot traffic, even on the coldest of days. As you already know, foot traffic is the best way to enhance the earning potential of the retail component of any given project, but also benefits all other use types.

Interior Design

Interiors are the spaces most seen by users. While the design of the architecture may be elegant, the interior Design is what completes the feeling of quality. Our approach to interior design is trend-free. Trends are short-lived. Decorish is a firm believer in minimal and clean design with well resolved functionality will never go out of style. Elegance in proportions, high quality, right materials and composition are the directions Decorish follow. Minimalism is far more difficult to pull off, compared to the old fashioned approach of hiding everything with decoration. What was once considered an opulent style, is now greatly reduced in value because of the proliferation of crystal chandelier and Louis XIV furniture. Simplicity is the new luxury.

Clear, uncluttered spaces are the way forward. Standing out is impossible when following trends. Due to Decorish’s strong belief that timeless, minimal design is the priority, we create memorable, distinguished spaces which often are the exact opposite of trends. The end result is clear, subtle and beautiful. As a result the projects exude a feeling of luxury, regardless if the construction budget is high or low. EDGE’s interior design surpasses market expectations, creating desirable products which stand out from the market.
